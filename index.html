Large Text Uploader - Ethereum

This tool uses transaction calldata to upload large batches of text to the Ethereum blockchain

Each transaction is limited to ~500k characters. If your transaction fails, split it into parts and try again.

There's a fee included of 0.0001 ETH per upload on top of the gas fee. That will show up in the value field of the transaction.
